# 🛡️ Shield Insurance Analysis Dashboard | Power BI

> An interactive Power BI dashboard built to analyze Shield Insurance's business performance through revenue, customer behavior, sales channels, and age group insights.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

The **Shield Insurance Analysis Dashboard** is an end-to-end Business Intelligence project developed using **Power BI**. The dashboard provides stakeholders with an interactive view of key business metrics, helping them monitor company performance, identify customer trends, evaluate sales channels, and support data-driven decision-making.

This project demonstrates the complete analytics workflow, including data cleaning, data modeling, DAX calculations, interactive dashboard design, and business storytelling.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

The dashboard was created to answer the following business questions:

- How is the company performing in terms of revenue and customer growth?
- Which cities contribute the highest revenue?
- Which age groups generate the most business?
- Which sales mode performs the best?
- How do revenue and customers change over time?
- What business insights can support strategic decision-making?

# 📌 Key Business Insights

### 💰 Revenue Performance

- Total premium revenue reached **₹989 Million**.
- Revenue increased by **18.4%** compared to the previous month, indicating strong business growth.

---

### 👥 Customer Growth

- The company serves approximately **27K customers**.
- Customer count increased by **18.3%**, reflecting healthy customer acquisition.

---

### 🌍 Geographic Analysis

- **Delhi NCR** contributes the highest revenue and customer base.
- Metropolitan cities continue to be the company's strongest markets.

---

### 🎯 Customer Demographics

- Customers aged **31–40** generate the highest revenue.
- The **41–50** age group is the second-largest contributor.
- Although the **65+** segment has fewer customers, it contributes significant premium revenue, indicating higher-value insurance purchases.

---

### 📈 Sales Channel Performance

- **Offline Agent** is the best-performing sales channel, contributing **55.67%** of total revenue.
- **Online Website** is the lowest-performing sales mode, contributing **12.60%** of total revenue.
- The business currently relies heavily on offline sales, highlighting an opportunity to strengthen digital channels.

---

# 💡 Business Recommendations

Based on the analysis, the following recommendations can help improve business performance:

- Continue investing in the **Offline Agent** network due to its strong revenue contribution.
- Improve digital sales channels, particularly the Online Website, through better customer experience and marketing initiatives.
- Focus marketing campaigns on customers aged **31–50**, as they generate the highest premium revenue.
- Strengthen customer retention strategies in high-performing regions such as **Delhi NCR**.
